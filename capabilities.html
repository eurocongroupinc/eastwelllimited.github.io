Vertical Integration

We control every step of the manufacturing process to ensure total quality management. Located in MIDC Rabale, our facility houses:

  • Weaving Division

    25 Heavy-duty needle looms capable of weaving fabrics up to 2 meters wide.

    50 High-speed 3/4 track braiding machines for gland packings and ropes.
